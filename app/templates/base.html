How to use

  • Step 1

    Click on "Select Your Photo" button, then select your photo.

  • Step 2

    Crop the image to select the product you want.

  • Step 3

    Click "Upload" button.

Currently the web app supports the following product's categories:

  • Clothing
  • Pets
  • Shoes
  • Watches
  • Home & Kitchen
